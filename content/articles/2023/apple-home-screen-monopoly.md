On iOS, all web apps launched from the home screen have to execute within a
webkit rendering context.

For developers trying to exist outside walled gardens the home screen is the
fulcrum that enforces Apple's monopoly.

I've read about how the EU is forcing apple to allow rendering engines other
then webkit. My hope is that we can hold the line and demand these rendering
engines have access to the home screen.

For me, this is the only sure path to a fair marketplace.
